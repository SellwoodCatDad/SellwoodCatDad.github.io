Wheeze's Bio:

Nicknames:

  1. Bug
  2. Psycho Pants

Story:

Wheeze has a collapsed rib cage. You can read more about it here. Essentially, this means that she like to play with her toys for about 5 minutes, and then has to break because of how hard her respiratory system is working.

She likes to scratch the damn furniture, so I'll spray her with a little mixture of water and vinegar when she does. This is a good deterrent for cats who like to scratch, by the way.

Anyways, we call her "Bug" because she will give you the biggest "bug-eyed" look when she's looking at you. She's very sweet, but very active, and is best friends with "Stain", or Wit. She idolizes Kitty, but Kitty doesn't seem to reciprocate
