Wit's Bio:

Nicknames:

  1. Stain
  2. Annoying Pants
  3. Pestus Maximus

Story:

You're probably thinking "Whoa, Kevin, why the harsh nicknames? Well, I have lost many hours of sleep because of this cat. I do love her very much, but she drives me crazy sometimes. I think it's partially due to her thinking I'm her actual dad. It is sweet, but I think this comes at a price.

Wit was found in Pullman, Washington by a runner along some railroad tracks. When found, she had infections in both of her eyes, was famished, and had other issues, too. If you look at the pictures of her you can see that the left eye is a bit strange. Well, that's because of the infections previously mentioned. In fact, she almost lost an eye or two, but the vet was able to successfully remove the infections, and get her in prime shape. As a result, she is mostly, if not completely, blind in the left eye and partially, to mostly, blind in the right eye.

She is the most annoying of all my cats, but is super affectionate, and drools when I pet her.

If you're in Sellwood, and you hear a cat chewing you out from the window, there's a good chance that it is indeed her.
